# Tic-Tac-Toe Glossary

- **Board**: The finite 3 by 3 grid of cells.
- **Cell**: A validated board position.
- **Mark**: The closed player mark set, `X` or `O`.
- **Move**: A command to place the next expected mark into an empty cell.
- **Game status**: The lifecycle state of a game: in progress, won, or draw.
- **Transition**: The pure rules decision that accepts or rejects a move against a game state.
